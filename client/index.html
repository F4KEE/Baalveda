About Baalveda

Baalveda Children's Health Care is a specialized pediatric clinic founded by Dr. Tanmay Pundlik Bangar and Dr. Shreya Tavhare Bangar. We focus on holistic wellness for children, combining modern pediatric science with traditional Ayurvedic therapies. Our antibiotic-free policy promotes natural healing and supports overall child development through nutrition, lifestyle, and proven Ayurvedic treatments.
